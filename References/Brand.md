Work with images & textures
Working with photos, illustrations and textures is just as important as the color palette. All these components together create new meanings, which are expressed through collages, animations or static visuals. To become a clear system, the chaos of visual forms is following the main rules: The color palette is limited, and new elements must obey the main components. Photos become duochrome due to combinations of corporate colors. An accent is a bright circle of color, texture or line. Even with restrictions like this variations are endless, but the overall style is endured.
