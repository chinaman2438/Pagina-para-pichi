# Distri-Med Website Renovation Plan  
**Objective:** Modernize the website to reflect Distri-Med’s position as a trusted medical equipment distributor in Mexico, improve product visibility (especially *Supor* and *Craniorehab*), and increase qualified inquiries from hospitals, surgeons, and distributors.

---

## 1. Strategic Goals

### 1.1 Strengthen Brand Positioning
- Communicate professionalism, reliability, and technical expertise.
- Present Distri-Med as a premium medical equipment provider.
- Align visual identity with modern medical industry standards.

### 1.2 Improve Product Visibility
- Feature **Supor** and **Craniorehab** prominently on the homepage.
- Reduce the number of clicks required to reach key products.
- Make product categories clear and intuitive.

### 1.3 Increase Lead Generation
- Improve calls to action (CTAs) across the site.
- Simplify the quotation request process.
- Optimize contact forms for B2B inquiries.

### 1.4 Modernize User Experience
- Implement a clean, responsive design.
- Improve navigation and site structure.
- Ensure mobile-first usability.

---

## 2. Homepage Renovation Objectives

### 2.1 Hero Section Redesign
- Replace outdated banner with a modern, high-resolution hero section.
- Include:
  - Clear value proposition headline (e.g., “Soluciones Avanzadas en Sistemas Médicos”).
  - Short supporting paragraph.
  - Strong CTA buttons:
    - “Ver Catálogo”
    - “Solicitar Cotización”
  - Rotating slides highlighting:
    - Supor
    - Craniorehab
    - Core product categories

### 2.2 Featured Products Section
- Add a “Productos Destacados” section below the hero.
- Include visual product cards with:
  - Image
  - Short description
  - CTA (“Ver Más”)
- Ensure Supor and Craniorehab appear here.

### 2.3 Category Overview Grid
- Add a clean icon-based grid showing major product categories.
- Each category should link to its dedicated page.

### 2.4 Trust & Credibility Section
- Display:
  - Certifications
  - Strategic partners
  - Years in business
  - Testimonials (if available)

---

## 3. Navigation & Structure Improvements

### 3.1 Simplified Menu Structure
Proposed structure:

Home

  - Neuro  
  - Maxilofacial  
  - Supor  
  - Craniorehab  
Servicios  
Nosotros  
Contacto  

### 3.2 Sticky Header
- Keep navigation visible while scrolling.
- Include:
  - Search bar
  - Quick contact button
  - Language toggle (if applicable)

---

## 4. Product Page Enhancements

Each product page must include:

- High-quality product images
- Technical specifications
- Clinical applications
- Downloadable brochure (PDF)
- Clear “Solicitar Cotización” button
- Contact information for sales representative

Optional enhancements:
- Product comparison
- Related products section
- Instructional videos (if available)

---

## 5. Mobile Optimization

- Fully responsive layout.
- Large tap-friendly buttons.
- Collapsible mobile navigation.
- Click-to-call and click-to-email functionality.
- Optimized loading speed.

---

## 6. Lead Generation & Conversion Optimization

### 6.1 Strong Calls to Action
- Add CTA buttons on:
  - Homepage
  - Product pages
  - Footer
- Use consistent wording:
  - “Solicitar Información”
  - “Cotizar Ahora”

### 6.2 Improved Contact Form
- Collect:
  - Name
  - Hospital/Clinic
  - Position
  - Phone
  - Email
  - Product of interest
- Keep form simple and professional.

### 6.3 WhatsApp Integration (Optional)
- Floating WhatsApp contact button for quick inquiries.

---

## 7. Visual & Design Modernization

- Clean typography (modern sans-serif).
- Professional medical color palette (blues, whites, light grays).
- Consistent spacing and layout.
- High-resolution product photography.
- Minimalistic, uncluttered interface.

---

## 8. Technical & SEO Improvements

- Optimize page speed.
- Structured product pages for SEO.
- Add meta descriptions and optimized headings.
- Implement Google Analytics & conversion tracking.
- Secure HTTPS and updated hosting if needed.

---

## 9. Expected Outcomes

After renovation, the website should:

- Clearly showcase Supor and Craniorehab on the homepage.
- Reflect a modern, professional medical supplier brand.
- Improve navigation and usability.
- Increase qualified inquiries and quote requests.
- Build trust with hospitals and healthcare professionals.
- Support long-term growth and digital marketing strategies.

---

## Final Vision

The renovated Distri-Med website should feel:

- Clean  
- Professional  
- Trustworthy  
- Technically strong  
- Easy to navigate  
- Conversion-focused  

It should position Distri-Med not as a simple distributor, but as a strategic medical solutions partner.
